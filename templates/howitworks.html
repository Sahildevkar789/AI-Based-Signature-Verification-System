The Verification Lifecycle

A three-stage process powered by computer vision and machine learning

01

Digital Enrollment

We collect multiple genuine signature samples during the student's initial enrollment. These samples are pre-processed and stored securely to create a robust character profile for comparison.

High-resolution scanning ensure micro-feature capture
02

Deep Feature Extraction

When a signature is submitted for verification, our AI applies binarization and morphological operations. It then extracts ORB (Oriented FAST and Rotated BRIEF) keypoints to identify unique structural characteristics.

ORB Algorithms detect keypoints invariant to rotation or scale
03

Consensus Classification

The similarity between the test signature and all reference genuine samples is calculated. This consensus score is then passed to a trained SVM (Support Vector Machine) model to classify the signature as GENUINE or FORGED.

Threshold-based logic ensures high reliability in fraud detection
