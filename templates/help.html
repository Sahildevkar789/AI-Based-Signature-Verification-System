Service Center & FAQ

Quick answers and support for your verification needs

What format should the signature images be?

Our system currently supports PNG, JPG, and JPEG. For optimal results, ensure the background is plain and the resolution is high enough to capture pen strokes.

How accurate is the verification?

The AI model (SVM) was trained using the industry-standard CEDAR dataset and has a tested overall accuracy of 91.27%.

Why was my signature rejected as FORGED?

Each signature has natural variations. If the similarity score is below the strict security threshold, it will be classified as forged to prevent unauthorized access. Try using a more consistent signature style.
